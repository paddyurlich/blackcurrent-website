What does Blackcurrent actually do?
We help businesses reduce energy costs and improve operational performance through intelligent energy systems. We design and install solar and battery solutions — and provide the software (Flex) to actively manage and optimise how energy is used across your site.
What makes Blackcurrent different from other solar providers?
Most providers stop at installation. Blackcurrent focuses on ongoing performance and value — ensuring your system continues to deliver financial returns through monitoring, optimisation, and control. The work doesn't stop at handover.
What is Flex?
Flex is Blackcurrent's energy management platform — providing real-time visibility, insights, and control over your system. It's not just monitoring. Flex is a decision and optimisation platform focused on financial outcomes.
How quickly will I see results?
You'll have immediate visibility into your system from day one. Optimisation gains compound over time as Flex learns your energy profile and continuously improves how your system performs.
Will I need to manage the system myself?
No. Automation handles the day-to-day. Flex runs in the background, continuously optimising battery charge, import/export, and loads — with optional manual control available if you want it.
Is Blackcurrent right for my business?
If energy is a meaningful cost in your business, the answer is likely yes. We work with SMEs, commercial and industrial businesses, and organisations with high or variable energy demand. We'll assess your site and tell you honestly what's possible.

About the Flex Platform
What can I see in Flex? ›
Flex gives you full visibility across your system: solar generation, consumption, import/export activity, battery performance, system health, and estimated costs and savings — all in one place, in real time.
Is Flex just monitoring? ›
No. Flex is a decision and optimisation platform. Monitoring is the foundation, but the real value is in what Flex does with that data — automatically adjusting your system to maximise financial performance, and surfacing insights that help you make smarter energy decisions.
Can I see the financial impact of my system? ›
Yes. Flex provides live financial dashboards that show cost savings, revenue, performance trends, and how your system is performing versus expectations. You get a clear picture of what your investment is actually delivering — in dollar terms.
Business Value & Cost Reduction
How does Blackcurrent reduce energy costs? ›
We reduce costs across multiple levers: solar generation, peak demand reduction, battery optimisation, tariff analysis, and load shifting. Flex actively manages all of these — not as a one-time configuration, but as an ongoing, continuously improving process.
Can I earn revenue from my system? ›
Yes — depending on your setup, you can generate revenue through energy export. Flex also opens up future opportunities to interact with the grid and participate in emerging energy markets as they evolve.
Can Flex help with electricity tariffs? ›
Yes. Flex analyses your current electricity plan and can compare alternatives — helping you identify the most cost-effective tariff structure for your usage patterns and system configuration.
Performance & Assurance
How do I know the system is working properly? ›
Flex compares actual system performance against expected benchmarks, and flags any discrepancies automatically. You always have a clear view of how your system is tracking — and Blackcurrent is monitoring alongside you.
What happens if there's an issue? ›
Blackcurrent proactively monitors your system and resolves issues before they become problems. You don't need to raise a ticket and wait — we're watching the data and will get in touch if something needs attention.

Installation & Technology
What gets installed at my site? ›
A typical Blackcurrent system includes solar panels, an optional battery (strongly recommended for maximum value), and an Edge device. The Edge device collects real-time data from your system and enables Flex to monitor and control it remotely.
What does the Edge device do? ›
Edge is the intelligence layer on-site. It collects performance data across your entire energy system and sends it to Flex — enabling real-time monitoring, automated control, and ongoing optimisation from our platform.
Will it work with my existing solar or energy systems? ›
Often, yes. Many existing systems can be integrated with Flex and the Edge device, allowing you to benefit from intelligent management without replacing your current infrastructure. We assess compatibility during onboarding.
